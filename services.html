Rates & Services

Base rate is $.013 per word.

Structural & Stylistic

I will provide structural and stylistic comments for what’s already working in the novel and what needs more work. I’ll look at aspects such as character development, plot structure, style, pacing, continuity, and the ultimate impact of the story. Some copy edits may be included, but this will not be the primary focus.

Copyedit

I will check written material for grammar, spelling, style, and punctuation issues. I may also suggest rewriting some sections, if necessary, to fix transitions, wordiness, jargon, and to ensure the style of the piece is continuous.

Proofread

When the material is nearly a finished product, meaning it has been edited, laid out, and designed, I will search the text for typographical errors. I will not suggest major changes to the text; rather, minor text and formatting errors to confirm the material is ready for publication.
